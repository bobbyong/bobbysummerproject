Free Tuition for Malaysian Students

TuitionX.com is a FREE online tuition centre for Malaysian students preparing for PMR and SPM examinations. There will be video lectures explaining the content of the Malaysian education syllabus followed by quizzes based on past PMR/SPM examination questions. TuitionX.com aims to be a high-quality alternative to neighbourhood tuition centres. At TuitionX.com, fellow Malaysian students can help their peers through a peer-to-peer learning experience. In the long run, TuitionX.com hopes to reduce the education inequality of students not being able to afford private tuition lessons.
